BREAKING NEWS March 5, 2025

Rewa Becomes First City in MP to Achieve 100% Digital Tax Collection

Rewa Municipal Corporation has achieved a milestone by becoming the first city in Madhya Pradesh to implement 100% digital tax collection system, making it easier for citizens to pay their municipal taxes online.

  • Online property tax payment system launched
  • Mobile app for instant payment and receipt generation
  • QR code based payment options available
  • 24/7 digital payment facility for citizens

  • Online assessment portal: www.rewa.gov.in/property-tax

  • 10% discount for early payment before March 20
  • Penalty charges will apply after March 31
